ABOUT

I fell in love with game development back in 2017, and I have been jumping between every hardware and software project I could imagine since then. From building a 3D printer to adding two numbers together in Rust and tackling every other programming puzzle I could get my hands on in between, I have had no shortage of interesting challenges. Fast forward a few years, and I have had the privilege of engineering solutions for an online retailer and a group of young entrepreneurs.

At the moment, I am looking to expand my horizons to as many fields of Information Technology and Computer Science as I can before I find the next step in my Software Engineering career. Currently I am designing operating systems at the University of South Australia, playing with sorting alogorithims on Brilliant, exploring data through Google's Data Analytics certificate, and tackling programming problems on LeetCode

When I am not at the computer, you might find me on a rock wall, in a gym chucking balls through or over nets, or hanging out with my family, partner, or two cats. Or coaching a group of talented young rowers at the Pulteney Boat Club on the Torrens River or watching them race along West Lakes.
